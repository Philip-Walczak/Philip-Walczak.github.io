About me

I am a 4th year PhD candidate the University of Washington School of Dentistry Department of Oral Health Sciences. Outside of lab, I practice dentistry at the Community Health Clinic of Snohomish County. During my undergraduate career, I was a student-athlete on UW's rowing team, where I won three national championships and a gold at the Henley Royal Regatta in Henley-on-Thames, England. In my free time, I enjoy the outdoors around beautiful Washington, pulling espresso shots, and photography.
